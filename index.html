Our Vision

Our aspiration is clear: to be acknowledged as the epitome of success in the funeral service industry. We tirelessly pursue this vision by adhering to a set of fundamental values that guide our every action.

Our Mission

At the heart of our mission lies a deep-seated commitment to our clients. We believe it is our solemn duty to fearlessly uphold their interests, providing them with innovative burial solutions that meet their unique needs. With a focus on timely service and unwavering support throughout their bereavement, we strive to exceed expectations at every turn.

Adapting to Change

We understand that the funeral care landscape is constantly evolving. That's why we have adapted our approach to meet the changing needs of our customers. Our mission remains steadfast: to provide quality funeral services at fair and reasonable prices, irrespective of class, race, or ethnicity.

Empowering Families

We believe that knowledge is power. Every family deserves access to free information and consultation to make informed decisions during the funeral planning process. Our team is dedicated to offering invaluable guidance, ensuring that our clients feel supported every step of the way.

Unparalleled Expertise

When you choose R2G, you can rest assured that you are receiving the highest quality funeral service available today. With three licensed funeral directors and two funeral preplanning consultants boasting over 50 years of combined experience, our team is equipped to personalize funeral services to meet your unique needs.

A Lasting Tribute

Whether you opt for a traditional burial or cremation service, we offer a range of merchandise options to create a meaningful tribute for your loved one. From elegant caskets to beautiful urns, we provide products to suit individual preferences and budgets.

Integrity and Professionalism

Above all, we conduct ourselves with honesty and integrity in all our dealings. We strive to earn and maintain the trust of our clients, colleagues, and the wider community through sincerity, responsibility, and accountability.

Value Statement

At our funeral home, we uphold principles of honesty and integrity in all our interactions with clients. We are deeply committed to providing bereaved families and the community at large with quality and style, ensuring that each individual receives the utmost care and respect during their time of need. Our aim is to foster trust by consistently demonstrating sincerity, responsibility, and accountability in our actions. We conduct ourselves with professionalism at all times, understanding that our conduct reflects not only on ourselves but also on the reputation of our establishment.

While we acknowledge the presence of other funeral homes in the area, we take pride in the fact that families who choose our services, whether in times of loss or for pre-arrangement, consistently encounter our exceptionally high standards of service. These standards encompass transparency in business administration and service delivery, ensuring that our clients receive nothing less than the best in their time of need.
