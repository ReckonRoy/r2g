Letting go of a loved one is often a challenging process. To provide your family with peace of mind, R2G Funerals cares deeply, allowing you to focus on comforting each other as you prepare to say goodbye to someone dear to you.

Though numerous decisions may arise, we assure you that we will guide you through every step of the process. We pledge to assist you in:

  • Designing the most fitting memorial or funeral service to honor their memory and pay tribute to their life achievements and experiences.
  • Selecting the most appropriate products, including a casket, burial vault, cremation urn, cemetery or mausoleum, and crematoria, to fit your budget and suit your needs and desires.
  • Crafting a fitting obituary to honor your loved one’s life experiences and social connections.
  • Welcoming your family and friends to our funeral home for your planned services, including a viewing, visitation, funeral, or memorial service.
  • Making all arrangements for the services, whether they are held at your place of worship, our funeral home, cemetery, or an alternate gathering place.
  • Providing you with beautiful print materials, including memorial photos and prayer cards, and creating a moving video tribute to be shown at the service. A copy of this tribute is given to the immediate family, and additional DVDs can be made for those who would also like a copy.
  • Offering an elegant hearse and funeral cars, as well as arranging escorts.
  • Assisting in the selection and on-site arrangement of floral tributes.
  • Providing access to grief education and support materials, as well as referrals to counseling services.
  • Preparing all paperwork, including vital record statistics, permits, filing and obtaining death certificates, and assisting with life insurance, veteran’s benefits, and Social Security claims.

We hope you will turn to us for compassionate care and impeccable service. Our pledge is to honor your loved one and family with dignity, respect, and personal attention to every detail. Call us today to explore the wide variety of choices available to you and your family. Alternatively, if your need isn’t immediate, feel free to send us an email.

In times of grief, individuals often experience a range of emotions, including sadness, anger, and confusion. Providing private spaces for small group meetings is essential to facilitate closure, offer social support, and celebrate the life of the departed. These purposes form the ultimate products of R2G RAINING GRACE FUNERAL PARLOUR (R2G). While traditional funeral homes have typically provided rituals for closure, they may not adequately address the need for social support in modern times. Our cooperative is structured to meet this need, emphasizing the importance of gathering for mutual support during the grieving process.
